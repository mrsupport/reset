EMAIL CHANGE

FOLLOW THESE INSTRUCTIONS TO CHANGE THE EMAIL:


  1. Navigate to the Epic Games Site
  2. Click on "Edit Email" and input your new email. Be sure to click "Continue" once you are done.
  3. Fill in the form below with the email address linked to your purchased account (i.e xxxxxx@mozmail.com).
  4. Wait for 10-20 seconds for your code to arrive or retry the process as needed.
  5. Don't forget to enter the email address from your purchased account.
